## Research — Cross-Chain Arbitrage
Ingested 3 academic papers + Biconomy MEE architecture:

### Paper 1: "Bunny Hops and Blockchain Stops" (IMDEA, 2025)
- 2.4B swaps, 34.8M bridge txs across 12 chains
- Only 10 multihop SDA instances found — multihop is dead
- 60% were profitable, power-law distribution
- Confirms: two-hop is the sweet spot

### Paper 2: "Cross-Chain Arbitrage: The Next Frontier of MEV" (Öz et al., TU Munich/Flashbots, 2025)
- 242,535 executed cross-chain arbs, $868.64M volume, $8.65M net profit
- 66.96% use inventory (9s median settlement) vs 33% bridges (242s)
- One address (0xCA74) captures ~40% of daily volume post-Dencun
- Loan-backed arb pattern: borrow on destination via Aave, arb, bridge back to repay
- Activity grew 5.5x over study period, spiked after Dencun

### Paper 3: "Cross-Rollup MEV" (Gogol et al., UZH/Oxford/Imperial, 2024)
- 500K+ unexploited arb opportunities on L2 rollups
- Price misalignments persist 10-20 blocks (7-420 seconds)
- Cross-rollup arb already exceeds DEX-to-CEX arb on L2s
- Spam strategy viable — cheap L2 gas means speculative txs work
- Focus: Arbitrum↔Base↔Optimism triangle
